01 — Introduction

This Is Not Just a Marketing Plan

This marketing plan is built around a simple principle: Marketing should not only create visibility — it should generate measurable revenue.

For Firebird Trading Systems — a platform built entirely around options trading through TradeStation — we are implementing a hybrid approach that combines structured marketing with direct user engagement to drive both immediate results and long-term growth. We call this approach the Revenue Activation & Growth Engine.

This system is designed to:

  • Drive consistent sales for tools, courses, and books bundle
  • Generate enrollments for group mentoring sessions
  • Build a strong community of traders around Firebird
  • Establish long-term authority and trust in the market

Instead of relying on a single channel, this approach integrates paid advertising, organic content, and active engagement with users across platforms. This ensures that we are not only attracting the right audience but also guiding them toward becoming paying customers.

In simple terms, we are building a system that: Brings people in → Engages them → Qualifies them → Converts them → Retains them.

This is not just a marketing plan. This is a structured revenue system designed to validate, grow, and scale Firebird Trading Systems efficiently.

Our marketing strategy is built to leverage these differentiators at every touchpoint — from the first ad a trader sees to the moment they join a mentoring group.

04 — Core Strategy

The Revenue Engine Framework

The marketing system for Firebird Trading Systems is built around a clear and structured flow:

Traffic
→
Engagement
→
Qualification
→
Conversion
→
Retention

1. Traffic Generation

We will bring targeted users into the Firebird ecosystem through paid advertising (Meta platforms initially), organic content across multiple social media platforms, and participation in options trading communities. The focus is on attracting individuals who are already trading options on TradeStation — or who are actively looking to learn structured options strategies.

2. Engagement Layer

Once users enter the system, the next step is active engagement. Instead of waiting for users to take action on their own, we will interact with users across platforms (comments, messages, discussions), answer questions and provide clarity, participate in relevant conversations within trading communities, and guide users toward the right entry points. This step builds trust and significantly increases the likelihood of conversion.

3. Qualification System

Before presenting paid offers, users will be guided through structured entry points: the Free Basic Course (video-based learning), a quick quiz to assess understanding and seriousness, and a waitlist system for group mentoring. This ensures that only relevant and interested users move forward, improving overall conversion quality.

Qualification Funnel Detail: After completing the free course, users take a short quiz. Based on their score and engagement level, they receive a personalized email sequence (5-7 emails over 14 days) that educates further and presents the most relevant paid offer — courses for learners, tools for active traders, or mentoring for serious users. Quiz completers who score above 70% are automatically invited to the mentoring waitlist. This segmentation ensures we're presenting the right offer to the right person at the right time.

4. Conversion Engine

Once users are qualified, they are directed toward the most suitable offer based on their needs and level: Courses for learning and skill development, Tools for active traders, Group mentoring for serious users, and Membership for long-term access and value. This personalized approach improves conversion rates and user experience.

5. Retention and Growth

After the first conversion, the focus shifts to maximizing long-term value: upselling relevant products and services, encouraging users to join membership programs, and keeping users engaged through continuous content and communication.

Specific retention mechanics include: monthly re-engagement email campaigns for inactive subscribers, a referral program offering 1 free month of tool access for each referred paying customer, quarterly "State of the Market" webinars exclusive to paying members, and churn-reduction sequences triggered when a subscriber's engagement drops below threshold levels.

This system operates as a continuous loop. Traffic brings in new users, engagement builds trust, qualification filters the right audience, conversion generates revenue, and retention increases lifetime value.

05 — Offers Structure

The Value Ladder

To ensure clarity, ease of decision-making, and higher conversions, all offerings under Firebird Trading Systems will be structured into clearly defined levels. Each offer is designed to either generate immediate revenue or move users deeper into the ecosystem.

1. Books Bundle — Entry Point

The five-book Mastering Campaign Trading series serves as the entry point for traders who prefer structured, self-paced learning. Available in multiple formats (eBook, paperback, hardback, audiobook), the bundle provides foundational knowledge across the full spectrum of options strategies and introduces users to Firebird's Campaign Trading methodology while generating initial revenue at a low commitment level.

2. Courses — Structured Learning

Basic Course (Free): Short video-based content designed to educate and engage users, includes a simple quiz to assess understanding. Advanced Course (Paid): Structured, in-depth training where users progress module by module with quizzes and guided learning flow. This creates a natural transition from free learning to paid education.

3. Trading Tools — Performance Enhancement

Firebird's custom TradeStation-integrated tools are a core value offering. Subscription-based access provides options traders with Greek-based analysis, position-finding tools, trade adjustment frameworks, and portfolio optimization — practical advantages designed for real options trading scenarios.

4. Group Mentoring — Guided Support

Group mentoring is a primary revenue driver. Small group sessions with limited seats, structured interactions on a weekly or bi-weekly basis, with entry through a waitlist system. This creates scarcity (limited availability), higher engagement, and predictable recurring revenue.

5. Membership — The Complete Solution

Membership acts as the complete Firebird ecosystem. Combines access to multiple offerings, provides ongoing value to users, and encourages long-term engagement. This is positioned as the highest-value offering for users who want full access and continuous improvement.

Where appropriate, bundled offers will be used to increase perceived value and encourage users to move toward higher-tier offerings.

06 — Paid Advertising Strategy

Controlled Growth, Data-Driven Scaling

Paid advertising will be used as a controlled and data-driven approach to generate consistent traffic and sales across Firebird offerings. Instead of relying on large upfront spending, we will start with a lean budget, test performance, and scale based on real results.

Initial Budget Approach

We will launch campaigns for all five core offers from day one — books bundle, course, tools, group mentoring, and membership. Each at $5/day, giving us $25/day total, approximately $750/month in ad spend. This is a separate cost paid directly to the ad platforms.

Running all five simultaneously gives us data across every segment of the audience from the start. We'll quickly identify which audiences respond to what, which creatives work best, and which messaging converts. As data comes in, underperforming campaigns get paused while winners get scaled.

Platform Strategy

We will initially focus on Facebook and Instagram (Meta Ads). These platforms provide advanced audience targeting, cost-effective testing capabilities, and access to a large and relevant trading audience. As we identify high-performing campaigns, we can expand into additional platforms such as YouTube.

Testing and Optimization

The initial phase will focus on testing and learning across multiple audience segments, different creatives (videos, images, messaging), and different offer angles. Based on performance, underperforming campaigns will be paused while high-performing campaigns will be optimized and scaled.

Retargeting Strategy

A large portion of users will not convert on their first visit. We will implement retargeting campaigns for website visitors, users who engaged with content, and users who showed interest but did not complete a purchase. This will significantly improve overall conversion rates.

Engagement and Follow-Up

Users generated through paid advertising will not be left unattended. We will actively engage with interested users through comments and messages, follow-ups where appropriate, and guide them toward the most relevant offer. This additional engagement layer improves conversion quality and speed.

The goal of paid advertising is not just traffic, but measurable outcomes: sales of books, courses, and tools, enrollments in group mentoring, and growth in membership subscriptions.

07 — Organic Marketing Strategy

Authority, Trust, and Consistency

Organic marketing will focus on building a strong, consistent, and trustworthy presence for Firebird Trading Systems across relevant platforms. The goal is to create visibility, establish authority, actively engage with users, and support conversions generated through paid efforts.

Platform Prioritization

Instead of spreading thin across seven platforms from day one, we will focus on three primary platforms in Month 1-2, then expand:

  • YouTube (Primary) — Long-form educational content, strategy breakdowns, campaign trading walkthroughs. This is where Firebird's depth of knowledge shines and builds lasting authority.
  • X / Twitter (Primary) — Real-time market insights, trading thoughts, and direct engagement with active traders. The trading community on X is highly engaged and conversion-friendly.
  • TikTok or Instagram Reels (Primary) — Short-form content to attract attention, simplify trading concepts, and drive traffic into the funnel.

Secondary platforms (activated Month 3+): LinkedIn for professional positioning, Facebook Group for community building, Reddit for organic engagement in trading subreddits.

Content Strategy

Content will be designed to educate, engage, and build trust with the audience. We will focus on short-form videos explaining trading concepts, market insights and observations, practical tips for traders, strategy breakdowns and examples, and educational snippets from courses.

Content Pillar Examples: (1) "60-Second Strategy Breakdown" — quick-hit videos explaining one options concept per post, such as Greeks, IV rank, or spread mechanics. (2) "Campaign Trading in Action" — weekly walkthrough of a real options trade setup using Firebird tools on TradeStation. (3) "Before & After: Trade Adjustments" — showing how Firebird's methodology handles spread adjustments when the market shifts. (4) "Market Positioning with Tom" — weekly options market outlook leveraging Tom's 30+ years of experience with complex spreads. These pillars ensure content is never random — every post ladders up to a conversion point.

Content Frequency

To maintain consistency and visibility: daily short-form content across primary platforms, regular long-form content on YouTube (minimum 1x/week), consistent posting and engagement on X, and active discussions within the community.

Engagement Approach

Organic marketing will not be limited to posting content. We will actively engage with users by responding to comments and messages, answering questions across platforms, participating in discussions within trading communities, and guiding users toward relevant entry points. This builds trust and improves conversion potential.

Over time, organic marketing will build a strong and recognizable brand presence, establish credibility within the trading space, create a consistent inbound flow of users, and reduce dependency on paid advertising.

08 — Email Marketing Strategy

The Conversion Engine Most People Ignore

Lead Capture Points

Every touchpoint in the Firebird ecosystem feeds into the email list: Free Basic Course signup, quiz completion, mentoring waitlist registration, books bundle purchase confirmation, website newsletter subscription, and lead magnets from social content (PDF strategy guides, checklists).

Segmented Nurture Sequences

Not all leads are equal. We will build separate email sequences based on entry point:

  • Course Viewers → 7-email sequence over 14 days educating on campaign trading fundamentals, then offering Advanced Course
  • Books Bundle Buyers → 5-email sequence sharing bonus insights from the books, then offering Tools trial
  • Quiz Completers (High Score) → 4-email sequence positioning Group Mentoring as the next step
  • General Subscribers → Weekly value newsletter with market insights, strategy tips, and soft CTAs

Ongoing Communication

Beyond automated sequences, the email list receives: a weekly "Firebird Market Brief" with Tom's insights and one featured tool/strategy, monthly "Community Spotlight" highlighting mentoring group wins and member success, and targeted promotional emails for new product launches, bundle offers, and seasonal campaigns. Email is where trust compounds over time — and where the highest-value conversions happen.
